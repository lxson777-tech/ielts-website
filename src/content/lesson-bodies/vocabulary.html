Why Vocabulary Matters in Every Paper

Where the Marks Are

  • Writing & Speaking: Lexical Resource is a full 25% of your band in both papers
  • Reading & Listening: every answer hides behind a paraphrase — the question rewords the text, and vocabulary is what lets you spot it
  • Examiners reward precision and collocation (“raise awareness”, “tackle congestion”), not rare words used awkwardly
  • Repeating the same basic words (good, bad, very, thing) caps Lexical Resource at Band 6

How to Learn It

  • Learn words inside a topic, with an example sentence — never as isolated translations
  • Record the collocation, not just the word: combat pollution, widen the gap
  • Build word families: sustain → sustainable → sustainability
  • Reuse new words the same week in a practice essay or speaking answer — that is what moves them into active vocabulary
