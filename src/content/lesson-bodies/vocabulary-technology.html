Topic Vocabulary

Technology & Society

Word / PhraseMeaningExample in Context
artificial intelligence (AI)computer systems that perform tasks requiring human-level intelligence"Artificial intelligence is transforming the healthcare and finance industries."
automationuse of machines/software to perform tasks previously done by humans"Automation has displaced many low-skilled manufacturing jobs."
digital divideinequality in access to digital technology and the internet"The digital divide remains a significant barrier to educational equality."
surveillanceclose monitoring of people, especially by governments or employers"Mass surveillance raises serious concerns about civil liberties."
data privacyprotection of personal information in digital systems"Tech companies must do more to protect users' data privacy."
innovationintroduction of new ideas, methods, or technologies"Technological innovation has driven unprecedented economic growth."
algorithma set of rules a computer follows to solve a problem"Social media algorithms can reinforce biased or extreme viewpoints."
misinformationfalse or inaccurate information spread unintentionally"The spread of misinformation online has become a major public concern."
cybersecurityprotection of computer systems from digital attacks"Investing in cybersecurity is now a national priority for most governments."
remote workingperforming job duties from home or a location outside the office"Remote working has fundamentally changed urban commuting patterns."

Key Collocations

  • rapid technological advancement / development
  • widespread adoption of technology
  • harness the potential of AI
  • pose a threat to privacy / employment
  • bridge the digital divide
  • regulate social media platforms

Useful Essay Phrases

  • "Technology has fundamentally transformed the way we…"
  • "While the benefits of automation are clear, the social costs cannot be ignored."
  • "Stricter regulation of social media is increasingly necessary because…"
  • "There is a real danger that AI will exacerbate existing inequalities."
  • "The challenge lies in harnessing the potential of technology while…"
